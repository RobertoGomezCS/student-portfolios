This is my semester project for CS 131 Spring 2026



Assignment description:

For my semester project, I created a presentation comparing some of the most popular Large Language Models out there, including ChatGPT, Claude, Gemini, and Grok. In the presentation, I explain what LLMs are, how they work at a basic level, and compare their strengths in different areas. The goal of the project was to give a simple and understandable overview of modern AI tools and discuss how these systems are impacting education, technology, and everyday life.
